TurboBard is a web app built to boost player immersion in tabletop RPGs like Dungeons & Dragons.

Users can search a database of hundreds of sounds, create groups, and fade seamlessly between them.

And it all happens quickly and smoothly enough that you can improvise with ease when the party goes somewhere unexpected.

The site runs smoothly on desktop and mobile, with a responsive front end design that promotes speed and customization, lovingly constructed with React.

I continue to maintain and update TurboBard for my own D&D sessions, and for the users around the world who regularly put it to good use.
